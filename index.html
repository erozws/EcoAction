AeroPure‑Grids: Smart Air‑Cleaning Drones

Our concept shows a network of self‑sufficient drones that clean polluted city air and share real‑time air‑quality data with communities, schools, and local government.

  • How it cleans: Biofilters and nanotech modules absorb CO₂ and other pollutants, releasing cleaner air.
  • Power & autonomy: Solar panels and micro‑wind turbines keep drones running day and night.
  • Smart targeting: AI sensors find hotspots and coordinate via a smart‑city grid.
  • Open data: Real‑time AQI maps are shared with the public for awareness and action.
  • Potential impact: With wide adoption, cities could cut air pollution by up to 60% in a few years.

Recommendations to Reduce Air Pollution

Transportation

  • Walk, bike, or use public transit; avoid idling.
  • Carpool and combine errands to reduce cold starts.
  • Keep tires inflated and engines maintained to cut emissions.

Home & Energy

  • Use LPG/induction over open burning for cooking; ventilate well.
  • Avoid burning trash; segregate waste and compost organics.
  • Choose efficient appliances and switch to clean energy when possible.

Community & Health

  • Check AQI before outdoor activities; wear masks during smog.
  • Plant trees and support no‑burn policies in schools and barangays.
  • Report smoky vehicles and industrial violations to authorities.
